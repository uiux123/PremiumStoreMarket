Vegetables Suppliers

Main Supplier

Badulla

Badulla District in Sri Lanka boasts fertile soil, perfect for cultivating a variety of vegetables. The vegetables grown in Badulla include cabbage, carrots, beans, leeks, and bell peppers, among others.

Second Supplier

Nuwara Eliya

Nuwara Eliya, located in the central highlands of Sri Lanka, is renowned for its high-altitude vegetable farming. The cool and temperate climate of Nuwara Eliya, with its mild temperatures and ample rainfall, provides an ideal environment for growing a variety of vegetables. Specializing in crops like carrots, leeks, potatoes, broccoli, and strawberries, Nuwara Eliya is known for its distinct vegetable varieties.

Third Supplier

Jaffna

Jaffna, located in the northern region of Sri Lanka, is renowned for its unique and flavorful vegetables cultivated in its sandy soils. Common vegetables grown in Jaffna include brinjal (eggplant), okra, snake gourd, and drumstick, among others. These vegetables are integral to Jaffna cuisine, adding depth and richness to traditional dishes like sambar, curry, and masala preparations.
