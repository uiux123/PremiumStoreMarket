Our Campaiens

Nugegoda Branch

Conveniently located in the bustling heart of Nugegoda, our branch offers a wide selection of fresh produce, pantry staples, and specialty items to cater to the diverse needs of our customers in the area.

Kandy Branch

Nestled in the picturesque city of Kandy, our branch provides a delightful shopping experience with a range of quality groceries, including locally sourced fruits, vegetables, and spices, reflecting the rich culinary heritage of the region.

Maradana Branch

Situated in the vibrant neighborhood of Maradana, our branch is a one-stop destination for all your grocery needs, featuring an array of premium products, international brands, and household essentials, ensuring convenience and satisfaction for our valued customers.
